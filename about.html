// About Tuga Hardware

Hard Shell. Long Life.

Tuga means tortoise. Not because we move slowly, but because the tortoise doesn't need to be fast. It survives because it's armoured, resilient, and built for the long game. That's the philosophy behind everything we sell.

We supply rugged tablets and handhelds to tradespeople across the United Kingdom. Electricians, plumbers, site managers, fleet operators, surveyors. People who need hardware that works on Monday and still works on Friday, covered in dust, rain, or worse.

What We Believe

Consumer tablets break. They crack on the first drop, overheat in direct sunlight, and die when you need them most. We think that's a problem worth solving.

Every product we stock is IP rated, MIL tested, and selected for real world conditions. We don't sell anything we wouldn't trust on a job site ourselves. No fragile glass slabs, no gimmicks, no compromise.

Why We Exist

UK tradespeople deserve better than choosing between overpriced enterprise contracts and consumer electronics that won't survive a week in the field. We sit in the gap: professional grade hardware, sold direct, at honest prices.

We keep things simple. Clear specs, transparent pricing, free UK delivery, and support from people who actually understand the products. If something goes wrong, we make it right.

Based in the UK

Tuga Hardware is a UK company serving UK tradespeople. All prices are in GBP, all deliveries ship to UK addresses, and our support team operates on UK time. No call centres, no runaround.

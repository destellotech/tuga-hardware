Bulk Orders & Team Discounts

Equipping a whole team? We make it simple. Every order with two or more units automatically receives a discount at checkout. No codes, no phone calls, no procurement forms. Just add your devices to the cart and the savings apply.

Tiered Discount Structure

The more you order, the more you save. Our discounts are applied automatically based on the total number of units in your cart.

  • 2 units: 3% off your entire order
  • 3 to 4 units: 5% off your entire order
  • 5 to 9 units: 8% off your entire order
  • 10+ units: 10% off, plus the option to request a custom quote

No minimum orders. No sales calls. No procurement forms. Just add to cart and the discount applies.

How It Works

Browse our full range of rugged tablets and add the devices your team needs to the cart. You can mix and match any combination of models. The discount is calculated on the total number of units, not per model.

For example, if you order three Tuga T8 tablets and two Tuga H6 handhelds, that counts as five units and qualifies for the 8% tier.

Orders of 10 Units or More

If you are ordering ten or more devices, you will receive the 10% discount automatically. You can also get in touch with our team to discuss a custom quote. We can help with larger fleet orders, scheduled deliveries, or specific configuration requirements.

Why Buy in Bulk from Tuga?

  • Automatic discounts with no coupon codes needed
  • Free UK delivery on every order, regardless of size
  • 12 month warranty on every device
  • 30 day returns, even on bulk orders
  • Mix and match any models in your order

Whether you are kitting out a crew of two or a fleet of fifty, the process is the same. Pick your devices, check out online, and we will handle the rest.
